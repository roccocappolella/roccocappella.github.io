Practice Activities

The practice activities in this course allowed me to further my knowledge of web development. Through hands-on exercises, I learned how to create a basic webpage, learned how to use CSS, implemented Bootstrap examples, and much more.

Individual Assignments

The individual assignments in this course let me apply the knowledge I gained through practice activities and the readings and develop it further. This allowed me to refine my ability to style the webpage with CSS,use grid layouts, and make the webpage responsive to various screens sizes.

Partner Labs

The partner labs in this course gave me the opportunity to apply what I learned in a collaborative setting. I developed teamwork skills by dividing tasks with my partner and combining our work to complete the webpage.

What I Learned

This course helped me develop practical web development skills that I can apply to real-world projects. Each assignment built upon previous lessons, which gave me anunderstanding of web development practices that I can use in the future.
