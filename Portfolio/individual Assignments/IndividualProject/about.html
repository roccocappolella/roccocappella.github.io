Our Story

Rocky's Rock Records is a newly opened record store in located in State College, PA. We pride ourselves on providing the best collection of rock and roll records from iconic bands like The Beatles, Eagles, Aerosmith, and more.

Why Choose Us?

We offer a wide selection of classic rock records, limited editions, and collectables. Our team is passionate about music and dedicated to providing our customers with the best records that music has to offer.
